How It Works

Congo offers brands the ability to leverage our groundbreaking technology to drive UGC campaigns by giving creators/influencers the power to shoot branded video on their smartphone and upload it across social media networks within minutes.

Complete Brand Control

Brand representative creates campaign via web portal, setting pre-defined video bumpers, watermark and meta-data.

Captured on the Go

Content creators shoot video on device, add title, description and tags; hit upload.

Cloud Production

Our servers automatically add campaign branding in the cloud.

Simultaneous Syndication

Within moments or at pre-scheduled intervals, branded video appears on youtube, facebook, twitter etc.

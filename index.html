Build Your Brand

Did you know that social video generates 1200% more shares than text and pictures combined? And that companies using video grow revenue 49% faster?

User Generated Content (UGC) has been growing exponentially in the last two years. Yet companies have only been able to capture and curate existing content on the web. This has been limiting for Brands giving them less control over the quality and creativity of their marketing campaigns.

Until now. Congo offers brands the ability to leverage our groundbreaking technology to drive UGC campaigns by giving creators/influencers the power to shoot branded video on their smartphone and upload it across social media networks within minutes.

Mobile First

Creators/Influencers shoot and upload the video from their smartphone. Our technology is easy to use and eliminates the need for additional hardware equipment and desktop software.

Complete Brand Control

Brand advocate creates campaign via web portal, setting pre-defined video bumpers, watermark and meta-data.

Captured on the Go

Content creators shoot video on device, add title, description and tags; hit upload.

Cloud Production

Our servers automatically add campaign branding in the cloud.

Simultaneous Syndication

Within moments or at pre-scheduled intervals, branded video appears on youtube, facebook, twitter etc.

Automated Post Production

Today, creating and posting branded video across social networks is time intensive requiring expensive video-editing software and post-production expertise. As a result, many of your average customers are not creating and contributing to UGC social media conversations.

Cloud Processing

Congo breaks through this barrier. Our technology does all the work in the cloud - adding bumpers and watermarks, normalizing the audio, exporting videos to the appropriate format, and pushing the video out to social networks.

Easy For Creators

Our intuitive platform allows customers with a variety of skill levels to create and post videos easily. Additionally, fans provide their content permission up front to the Brand. As a result, Congo expands your reach and amplifies the voice of all your customers to the world.

Extend Your Reach

With Congo, branded video is pushed out simultaneously across all selected networks as native video. Your company will enjoy 10x higher reach than non-native video.
